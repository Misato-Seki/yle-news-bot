Eero Heinäluoma, a Finnish MEP, has said he will take part in Saturday's Pride parade in Budapest. The parade has been banned by Hungarian police. "I want to show my support for the rights of gender and sexual minorities by participating in the Pride march," he said. The Hungarian government, led byViktor Orban, has limited the visibility of sexual minorities in society.
