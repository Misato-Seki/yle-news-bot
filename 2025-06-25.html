THL: One in four adults in Finland is significantly obese

https://yle.fi/a/74-20169421

2025-06-25

Last year, 24.5 percent of adults in Finland had a body mass index (BMI) of 30 or higher. The proportion of obese people has increased in most wellbeing services counties across Finland. In 2024, the highest obesity rate was in the South Ostrobothnia welfare area, where just over 30 percent of the population was obese. Helsinki recorded the lowest obesity rates in 2020, 2022, and 2024.
