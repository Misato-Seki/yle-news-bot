Finnair pilots finally reach contract deal

https://yle.fi/a/74-20166481

2025-06-08

The pilots' union SLL reached a collective bargaining agreement on Sunday with the Service Sector Employers' Association (Palta), which represents Finnair. The negotiated result covers three years, with the last year optional. The three-year deal is broadly in line with other recent labour contract decisions. No agreement has yet been reached in separate labour talks covering the airline’s ground services, another long-running dispute.

One killed, four injured in multiple-vehicle crash in Kurikka

2025-06-08

A middle-aged man died and two people were seriously injured in the accident in the village of Jalasjärvi, about 100 kilometres from Vaasa. The incident began when a passenger car coming from the direction of Tampere drifted into the oncoming lane for unknown reasons and collided head-on with a van. Highway 3 was closed to traffic for an hour and a half due to the accident.

Many low-income families struggle to cope during summer holidays

https://yle.fi/a/74-20166444

2025-06-08

Schoolchildren’s summer holidays put many disadvantaged families into even more dire financial straits. Many municipalities, parishes and sports clubs offer free activities, including summer camps. To help with acute food insecurity in children, many cities offer free hot park lunches for youngsters. Save the Children offers summer holiday activities for children, for example, and parents can turn to MLL for support.
