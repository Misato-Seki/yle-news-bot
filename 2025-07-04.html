Appeals court upholds life sentence for Valkeakoski murderer

https://yle.fi/a/74-20171140

2025-07-04

The Turku Court of Appeal has upheld the life sentence of Tuomas Sakari Matias Salminen, born in 2005, for the rape and murder of a 15-year-old girl in Valkeakoski. The defence had sought to have the charges downgraded and to reduce the emotional damages awarded to the victim's family. The complainants, meanwhile, asked the court to uphold the original ruling.

Three sentenced to prison on counterfeiting charges

https://yle.fi/a/74-20171081

2025-07-04

Finnish court sentences three men to prison for aggravated counterfeiting. They were found guilty of attempting to circulate tens of thousands of euros in forged currency. The forged banknotes, valued at over 180,000 euros, were shipped from France to Finland. One of the convicted individuals was ordered to forfeit nearly 23,000 euro in cash found in his possession. The verdict is not yet legally binding and may be appealed.

23-year-old admits to Tampere stabbings, motive unclear

https://yle.fi/a/74-20171044

2025-07-04

A 23-year-old Finnish man is being held on suspicion of four counts of attempted manslaughter. Four adults, first three women and then a man, were stabbed near the Ratina shopping mall in central Tampere after 4pm on Thursday. The charge could be upgraded to attempted murder, officials said. The suspect has a previous criminal record, including assaults, with the most recent conviction in June.

Summer storm Ulla knocks out power to thousands of households

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170985

2025-07-04

More than 19,000 households were without electricity on Friday afternoon as a storm dubbed Ulla swept inland from the southwest. In Keuruu, Central Finland, more than half of the population was without electricity in the early afternoon. The Satakunta Rescue Department had the most emergency calls in Rauma, Eurajoki and Pori. The storm also hit Southwest Finland with force, with first responders carrying out some 60 damage control tasks overnight.
