If you live in Finland, you're likely to get a mental disorder diagnosis at some point

https://yle.fi/a/74-20169859

2025-06-27

Mental health challenges affect most people at some point – either personally or through a loved one. The most common issues are anxiety and mood disorders, particularly depression. Males are most often first diagnosed around the age of six, and women between the ages of 15 and 19. The high number of diagnoses could be explained by the significant increase in treatment services in recent decades and a lower threshold for seeking treatment.

EK: Growing confidence in manufacturing and services; job cuts likely in construction

https://yle.fi/a/74-20169832

2025-06-27

Confederation of Finnish Industries (EK) said on Friday that its manufacturing confidence index was up slightly in June. Confidence in the sector sank to –33 in June, down from –30 in May, both far lower than the long-term average of –9. In May, construction sector confidence in Finland was the weakest in the entire EU. On the other hand, confidence in the retail sector weakened in June after a few months of growth.
