Increase in domestic violence reports

2025-06-13

In 2024 there were some 13,000 reports of domestic or intimate partner violence affecting some 12,500 victims. Approximately 74 percent of the victims were adult women. The number of underage victims, and reports of violence perpetrated by parents on children, both fell year-on-year. Investigation processing times for family and domestic violence, however, have increased, according to Statistics Finland. The total number of victims increased by two percent on the previous year.
