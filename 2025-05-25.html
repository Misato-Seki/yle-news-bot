Saimaa canal sailing season opens

https://yle.fi/a/74-20163911

2025-05-25

The Saimaa Canal will reopen for seasonal traffic on Monday. Freight and passenger vessels can pass through the canal until the end of December. recreational cross-border boating between Finland and Russia remains prohibited. The restrictions remain in place due to EU-imposed sanctions and decisions by the Finnish government. Finland first obtained a 50-year lease on the then-Soviet Union part of the canal in 1963, with the contract renewed for an additional 50 years in 2013.
