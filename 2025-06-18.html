Employee or entrepreneur? Wolt wants to let couriers decide

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168524

2025-06-18

Finnish food delivery firm Wolt has drafted a response to a ruling by the Supreme Administrative Court. Court ruled that the company's couriers should be considered employees and not self-employed entrepreneurs. Wolt plans to let the couriers themselves decide whether they want to be an entrepreneur or an employee. Currently Wolt couriers are paid per delivery, but the amount varies based on several factors — including distance travelled, value of the order, and the time of day.

EVA survey: Finns dissatisfied with healthcare

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168491

2025-06-18

Finns give a poor grade to the Finnish healthcare system, according to an opinion poll. Just under a third of respondents said that the system works well, and more than half said it was poor. People were especially dissatisfied with the difficulty in getting access to treatment. The poll again saw support for a so-called "freedom of choice" model in healthcare, in which patients could choose between public sector and private sector providers.

Satellite images reveal Russia’s military town

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168262

2025-06-18

Satellite images obtained by Yle show how Russia began extensive construction work last winter in a closed military town, across the border from Finnish Lapland. Major excavation work has been carried out and several new buildings have been erected at the Lupche-Savino garrison. Russia has two reasons to increase military activity near Finland. The first and more urgent reason is to train military personnel and service older equipment so it can be used for the war in Ukraine.
