Finland hosts multinational air force exercise

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168039

2025-06-16

The Finnish Air Force will host the multinational Atlantic Trident 25 air exercise from 16 to 27 June. The Atlantic Trident exercise is held every two years, involving the air forces of the United States, the United Kingdom and France. Finland's hosting of the exercise this year marks the first time it has been held outside either of the three other participating nations. The exercise will involve more than 40 aircraft and approximately 1,000 personnel members.
