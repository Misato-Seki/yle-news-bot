Finland backs EU's 2040 emissions reduction target, says climate minister

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170743

2025-07-02

Finland supports European Commission's proposal to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 90 percent by 2040. The emissions target is part of the EU's broader roadmap to achieving climate neutrality by 2050. Minister of the Environment and ClimateSari Multala said the proposal aligns with Finland's key priorities in climate diplomacy. Multala added that the Finnish government will finalise its official position and deliver a communication to Parliament by early September.

Major reform slashes housing aid for thousands of Finnish students

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170649

2025-07-02

Housing support for Finnish students is set to be cut from August. Students renting from their parents will be among the hardest hit. The change stems from a broader overhaul of student housing support. The reform is expected to save the government more than 5 million euros annually. The study grant housing supplement will only be paid during months when the student is enrolled in studies. The other option is to find work during the academic year.
