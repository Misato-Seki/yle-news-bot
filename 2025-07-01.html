Strawberry harvests start in Southern Finland, prices vary depending on location

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170554

2025-07-01

Finnish strawberries have been in short supply and their prices have been relatively high. However, relief is on the horizon for Finland's many strawberry fans, as harvests have started in Southern Finland. Strawberries are already being harvested at a rapid pace across the country, including in Southern and Southwest Finland, Häme, Pirkanmaa, Satakunta and the southern parts of Eastern Finland. A five-kilo box of strawberries costs between 40 and 45 euros per liter.

Finland's consumer prices rose by 1.9% in June

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170478

2025-07-01

Finland's consumer prices in June rose by 1.9 percent, year-on-year, according to preliminary figures from Statistics Finland. Last month's harmonised index of consumer prices (HICP) eased slightly compared to May, when the rate stood at 2.0 percent. The biggest price increases were seen in health care costs, which ticked up by 7.3 percent compared to June 2024. The cost of alcoholic beverages and tobacco saw the second-largest hikes, increasing by around four percent.

Survey: Women, youth most doubtful about pension security

https://yle.fi/a/74-20170397

2025-07-01

A year ago, 20 percent of Finns said they expected to manage well in retirement. Now only five percent feel the same. Women have less confidence than men that their pension will cover a decent retirement. People under 30 are more likely than others to rate their future retirement income as poor or fairly poor. The pension reform agreed in January received an overall positive response. More than half viewing it as at least fairly good.
