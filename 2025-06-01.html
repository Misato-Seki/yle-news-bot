Orpo: West must intensify pressure on Russia to end war in Ukraine

https://yle.fi/a/74-20165140

2025-06-01

Finnish Prime Minister Petteri Orpo has called for increased pressure on Russia from the West. Orpo stressed that more sanctions are needed to force Russia toward a ceasefire and peace process. He also described the suffering caused by Russia in Ukraine as "shocking" Orpo was also asked about increasing Finland's defence spending, a topic that will feature prominently in the coming week as Nato defence ministers meet in Brussels.

Fewer Finnish parents buying smartphones for grade schoolers

https://yle.fi/a/74-20165125

2025-06-01

Finnish parents increasingly reluctant to give young children smartphones. Just 36 percent of children turning seven now have a smartphone, down from over 70 percent in the previous year. Both Elisa and DNA now recommend that children under the age of nine should not be given a smartphone. Study shows too much screen time is linked to reduced physical activity, poorer mental health, and lower academic performance in children.

Police disperse youth gatherings across Finland amid end-of-school festivities

https://yle.fi/a/74-20165112

2025-06-01

In Helsinki, police estimated 2,000 to 3,000 youths gathered in Kaivopuisto park. In Oulu, the situation escalated in Kiikkeli, where hundreds of young people gathered. In Espoo and Lohja alone, dozens of litres of alcohol were seized. In Lahti, officers intervened in a number of assaults involving minors at the harbour area. In Tampere, an estimated 4,000 people gathered at Rosendahl beach.
