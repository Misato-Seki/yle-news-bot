Encryption-breaking and beyond — Finland to lead EU defence project on quantum technology

https://yle.fi/a/74-20164884

2025-05-30

Finland has been named to lead an EU project on the use of quantum technology for defence purposes. Joining Finland on the project will be Germany, Denmark, Latvia and Italy, with Sweden, Greece and the Netherlands as observers. The main focus areas will be how quantum technology can be used in positioning, territorial surveillance and air and missile defence. So far no details have been revealed about the project’s budget or practical implementation.

Metsähallitus: Record high number of Saimaa ringed seal pups born this spring

https://yle.fi/a/74-20164955

2025-05-30

A total of 111 Saimaa ringed seal pups were born in the first half of 2025. Wildlife agency Metsähallitus is working with WWF Finland to make the region as optimal as possible for the seals' breeding season. This includes the building of hundreds of so-called 'artificial snowdrifts', which help to protect pups against predators as well as the cold in the crucial first few weeks after birth.
