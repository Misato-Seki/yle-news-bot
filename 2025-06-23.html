Study: Short sick leaves cost municipalities over €130m a year

https://yle.fi/a/74-20169028

2025-06-23

Short sick leaves cause significant expenses for municipal employers. Absences lasting up to 10 days account for over half of the total annual sick leave costs. In 2021, municipal employers incurred 135 million euros due to these short-term absences. Short absences appear not only as an economic burden but also as a potential warning sign of workload and management challenges. The study draws on data from 215,000 employees collected by the state pension agency Keva.
