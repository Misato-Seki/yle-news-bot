Study: Most Ukrainians don't want to leave

https://yle.fi/a/74-20167868

2025-06-15

Most working-age Ukrainians in Finland under temporary protection have come here to stay. Researchers at OsloMet University in Norway conducted a study interviewing Ukrainians who arrived in Finland, Sweden, Norway and Denmark. Only nine percent of Ukrainians said they want to return to their home country after the war. Nordic governments will face a difficult decision in the future: will Ukrainian refugees be allowed to stay, or will they be pressured to return?
