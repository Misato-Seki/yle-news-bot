Study: Finland third-most expensive country in EU for alcohol last year

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168791

2025-06-19

Finland was the third most expensive country in Europe to buy alcohol last year. Only Iceland and Norway had higher prices for booze, but neither are EU member states. Finland was also found to be 10 percent above the EU average for the prices of food and non-alcoholic beverages. In terms of tobacco prices, Finland was the sixth most expensive nation in Europe last year, according to Eurostat.

The IAU said that it would to resume negotiations with the employers' group next week.

MPs approve Finland's withdrawal from Ottawa landmine treaty

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168711

2025-06-19

MPs approved the measure to withdraw from the agreement by a vote of 157 to 18. Those voting against the measure included 10 MPs from the Left Alliance, seven Greens MPs, as well as one from the Swedish People's Party. The treaty bans the use of, stockpiling, production and transfer of anti-personnel mines. In 2012, Finland became the last EU member state to sign onto the 1997 treaty.

Finland approves reform of long-contested Sámi Parliament Act

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168666

2025-06-19

Finland's parliament voted in favour of changes to the Sámi Parliament Act. The motion was carried on a vote of 150 for and 27 against. Efforts to pass the legislation have failed during each of the previous three parliamentary terms. Those opposed to the reform argued the definition of voting entitlement was too strict. Those in favour said it was too loose — leading to years of debate and division over the issue.

Anjala is Finland's hottest spot at Midsummer

https://yle.fi/a/74-20168665

2025-06-19

Yle asked the Finnish Meteorological Institute (FMI) to find out where in Finland is statistically the warmest place to spend Midsummer. The answer depends on what you're looking for. The highest average daily high on 23 June over the period in question was recorded at Anjala, which is now part of the city of Kouvola. If the highest daily average temperature is the determining factor, then Helsinki-Vantaa airport is warmest spot.
